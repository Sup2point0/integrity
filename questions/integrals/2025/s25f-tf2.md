## Solution

```math
\begin{align*}
  &\ \int_{0}^{1}\frac{x\left(2-3x\right)}{3}+\tan\left(x-\frac{1}{2}\right)\ dx
  \\ =&\ \int_{0}^{1}\frac{2x\left(1-\frac{3}{2}x\right)}{3}+\tan\left(x-\frac{1}{2}\right)\ dx
  \\ =&\ 0
\end{align*}
```
