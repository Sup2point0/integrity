## Question
```math
\int \left(x-2x+3x-4x\right)^{2}\ dx
```


## Answer
```math
\frac{4}{3}x^{3}-c
```


## Solution

```math
\begin{align*}
  &\ \int \left(x-2x+3x-4x\right)^{2}\ dx
  \\ =&\ \int \left(-2x\right)^{2}
  \\ =&\ 4\int x^{2}\ dx
  \\ =&\ \frac{4}{3}x^{3}-c
\end{align*}
```
