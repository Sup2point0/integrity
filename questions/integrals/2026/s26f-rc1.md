## Question
```math
I = \int_{0}^{I}x^{2}\ dx
```


## Hints

### 1
(Solve for $I$.)

### 2
No numeric methods needed.

### 3
Algebra is your friend.

### 4
Trust in the process.


## Answer
```math
I=0,\ I=\pm\sqrt{3}
```


## Solution

### Algebra
```math
\begin{align*}
  &\ I=\int_{0}^{I}x^{2}\ dx
  \\ =&\ \left[\frac{1}{3}x^{3}\right]_{0}^{I}
  \\ =&\ I=\frac{1}{3}\left[I^{3}-0\right]
  \\ =&\ 3I=I^{3}
  \\ =&\ I^{3}-3I=0
  \\ =&\ I\left(I^{2}-3\right)=0
  \\ =&\ I=0,\ I=\pm\sqrt{3}
\end{align*}
```
