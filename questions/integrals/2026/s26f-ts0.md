## Solution

```math
\begin{align*}
  &\ \int_{dit}^{lz}idx
  \\[0.5em] =&\ i\int_{dit}^{lz}dx
  \\[0.5em] =&\ i\left[x\right]_{dit}^{lz}
  \\[0.5em] =&\ i\left[lz-dit\right]
  \\[0.5em] =&\ ilz-\left(i\right)^{2}dt
  \\[0.5em] =&\ liz+dt
\end{align*}
```
